About Me

I am a data professional, proficient in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. My workflow focuses on SQL and Excel to collect, clean and transform raw datasets to uncover trends, patterns, and KPIs across sales, customer behavior, and operational performance. I build interactive PowerBI dashboards that visualize key metrics and applying analytical techniques like RFM segmentation to support data-driven stories that help organizations make informed decisions.

With experience in Power BI, SQL, and data modeling, I transform complex data into a clear, comprehensive report that reveal critical areas. This portfolio showcases my projects across various sectors.
